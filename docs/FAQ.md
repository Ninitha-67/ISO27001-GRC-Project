# FAQ

## How do I add a new risk?
Add a new row in `Risk Register` and fill in the dropdown fields.

## Why are some cells blank?
The workbook leaves formula cells blank until valid input is entered.

## How are risk levels calculated?
`Likelihood x Impact` determines the score, which maps to Critical, High, Medium, or Low.

## Can I change the dropdown values?
Yes. Update the `Lists` sheet.

## Why does the control list matter?
It gives a reference point for mapping risks to ISO controls and tracking coverage.
